Control+Alt+Deplete

Creative Technology Project

Project Overview

Control Alt Deplete is a creative technology project that explores digital consumption and its impact on our daily lives. The project combines interactive elements with visual storytelling to create an immersive experience that challenges viewers to reflect on their relationship with technology.

Technical Details

  • Interactive Installation
  • Real-time Data Visualization
  • Custom Software Development
  • Physical Computing

Project Goals

The primary goal of Control Alt Deplete is to raise awareness about digital consumption patterns and their effects on our daily lives. Through interactive elements and data visualization, the project aims to create a space for reflection and discussion about our relationship with technology.
