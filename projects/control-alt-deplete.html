Control+Alt+Deplete

Interactive Sound Sculptural Installation

Project Overview

An interactive sound sculptural installation that brings to life the image of a solitary tree mutated with electronic waste, emerging from a landfill. Constructed entirely from discarded electronics—keyboards, wires, broken monitors, motherboards, and phone fragments—alongside other post-consumer trash, the tree will stand as a haunting monument to our age of over consumption and obsolescence. Project Website

Technical Details

  • Custom design and fabrication using recycled electronics and natural materials
  • Live coded audio using Strudel.js
  • Arduino controlled sensors and LED lights
  • Processing based controller for serial input and Midi output
