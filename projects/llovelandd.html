LL❤️VELANDD

Album - Original Music

Project Overview

LLOVELANDD is an album of original music written, performed, and recorded during the isolation of the pandemic. This collection of love songs captures moments of vulnerability, hope, and emotional complexity through stripped-down arrangements and heartfelt lyrics. Blending lo-fi textures with melodic intimacy, the album reflects a personal and reflective creative process shaped by distance, uncertainty, and the enduring need for connection. Listen on Bandcamp
