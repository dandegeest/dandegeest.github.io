Deeds & Co Resource Recovery

Songwriting,Live Performance and Generative AI

Project Overview

Resource Recovery EP is a concept-driven collection of original music exploring themes of transformation, sustainability, and personal renewal. Inspired by the process of reclaiming value from waste, each track reflects a stage in the journey of recovery—both environmental and emotional. Combining generative AI tools, field recordings, and layered production techniques, the EP bridges the sonic and thematic space between discarded material and creative rebirth. Originally developed as part of a larger multimedia capstone project, the EP was performed live in its entirety and serves as both a narrative arc and a call to reflect on what we throw away—and what we choose to reclaim.
