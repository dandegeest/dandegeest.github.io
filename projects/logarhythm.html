LogaRhythm

Project Description

Project Overview

LogaRhythm is a collaborative digital music experiment developed by a four-person creative team, blending innovative design with custom-built technology to craft an immersive, multisensory experience. At its core is a generative MIDI sequencing engine that drives both sound and synchronized visual output in real time. Custom visuals are displayed on four reclaimed LCD screens arranged in a campfire formation, simulating a flickering, digital hearth. Reactive lighting further enhances the atmosphere, responding to the rhythm and tone of the audio. The project explores the intersection of mathematics, music, and communal interaction, inviting participants to engage with a system where code, sound, and visual expression harmonize in a living, breathing composition.
