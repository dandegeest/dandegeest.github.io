aMiRRoR MySubconscious

Generative AI Art Installation

Project Overview

aMiRRoR is an interactive art installation that reimagines the act of looking at oneself. Using real-time AI image processing, the installation captures a viewer's image and transforms it into a surreal or expressive reinterpretation, displayed on an LCD screen in place of a traditional reflection. By distorting, abstracting, or recontextualizing the viewer's likeness, aMiRRoR invites participants to question identity, perception, and the influence of technology on self-imageâ€”turning a mirror into a dynamic portal for introspection and transformation.
