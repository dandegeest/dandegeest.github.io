About DD

I am a Creative Technologist and Full Stack Developer with a passion for merging art and technology. My work spans interactive installations, digital art, and software solutions that range from experimental creative tools to large-scale production systems for major companies. I hold a master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction and a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, combining technical depth with a strong focus on user-centered design and UX. I strive to create meaningful experiences that challenge perceptions, solve real-world problems, and inspire creativity across both artistic and traditional software domains.
